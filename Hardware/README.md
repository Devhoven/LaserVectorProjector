# LVP Hardware
  
### Prebuilt components:
- Arduino Due 
- 24V 0.68W Fan
- Red Laser 5mW (635nm)
- PTA-40 Kit 
> **Including:** <br>
> Bipolar 24 V Power supply <br>
> 2x Galvo drivers <br>
> 2x Galvos <br>
> Galvo mount <br> 
> A few cables
### Electrical components:
- IRLZ14 MOSFET 
- MCP4822 DAC
- 2x LM358 op amps
- 11x 1k立 Resistors
- 2x 1.5k立 Resistors
- 2x 500立 Resistors
- 2x 100立 Resistors
